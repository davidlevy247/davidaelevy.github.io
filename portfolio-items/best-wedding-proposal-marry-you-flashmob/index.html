Project Description

The most-watched proposal flashmob in Las Vegas of locals.
Vartan from VZ Dance Studios here in Las Vegas hired me to produce this video.
He waited seven years before asking his girlfriend Tania to marry him, an instructor at his studio.
Vartan hired a fake bride and groom, and Tania was tasked with choreographing them a flashmob.
Tania had no idea the flashmob she choreographed was for her or that Vartan would ask her to marry him that day.
I scrambled a local team and we produced this video.
